About Five Seasons Radon

8+ years of protecting Iowa families from dangerous radon gas. Licensed, insured, and committed to your safety.

Who We Are

Five Seasons Radon is a locally owned and operated radon mitigation company serving Cedar Rapids, Linn County, and surrounding Iowa communities. With over 8 years of hands-on experience, we specialize in testing and eliminating dangerous radon gas from homes and businesses throughout Eastern Iowa.

We got into this business for one reason: to protect families. Iowa consistently ranks among the top states for radon exposure, and Cedar Rapids is no exception. Linn County homes frequently test above the EPA action level of 4 pCi/L, putting families at risk every single day they go without mitigation.

Our team of certified radon professionals uses proven, industry-standard techniques — primarily sub-slab depressurization systems — to reduce radon levels quickly and effectively. Every installation is backed by our guarantee to bring your radon levels below 4 pCi/L, and we include post-mitigation testing to verify results.

Our Commitment to Iowa Families

In Cedar Rapids and throughout Linn County, we see homes testing at dangerous levels every single week. That's why we're committed to making professional radon mitigation accessible and affordable for every family in our community.
