Professional Radon Mitigation Services in Cedar Rapids

Cedar Rapids is the second-largest city in Iowa and the seat of Linn County, home to more than 137,000 residents spread across neighborhoods from the Czech Village and New Bohemia districts to the growing southwest side. The city's geology — primarily glacial till and limestone bedrock — creates ideal conditions for radon gas to accumulate beneath homes and commercial buildings. According to the EPA, Linn County falls squarely within Zone 1, the highest-risk designation for radon exposure, meaning the average indoor radon level is predicted to exceed 4 pCi/L. For Cedar Rapids homeowners, professional radon testing and mitigation is not optional — it is essential for protecting your family's long-term health.

Our team provides comprehensive radon mitigation throughout Cedar Rapids, from older homes in the historic Wellington Heights and Mound View neighborhoods to newer construction in Tiffin and along Edgewood Road. We install EPA-recommended active soil depressurization systems that reduce radon concentrations by up to 99 percent. Every installation begins with a thorough diagnostic assessment of your home's foundation type — whether it is a poured concrete basement, block wall foundation, or slab-on-grade construction — so we can engineer the most effective and discreet system for your property. We are licensed and insured, and we stand behind every installation with post-mitigation testing to confirm your radon levels have dropped below the EPA action level of 4.0 pCi/L.

Radon is the leading cause of lung cancer among non-smokers in the United States, responsible for an estimated 21,000 deaths each year. In Cedar Rapids, the combination of cold Iowa winters and tightly sealed homes means radon can accumulate to dangerous levels quickly. During heating season, the stack effect draws soil gases — including radon — upward through foundation cracks, sump pits, and utility penetrations. Whether you are buying a new home near Prairie Park or renovating a property in the Time Check neighborhood, a professional radon test should be your first step. If levels come back elevated, our mitigation systems can typically be installed in a single day with minimal disruption to your household.

We proudly serve all of Cedar Rapids and surrounding Linn County communities including Marion, Hiawatha, Ely, Center Point, Mount Vernon, and Lisbon. Our local knowledge means faster response times and service from technicians who understand the specific soil conditions and building practices common to eastern Iowa. Call us today for a free estimate and take the first step toward a safer home in Cedar Rapids.
