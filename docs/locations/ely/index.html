Protecting Ely Families from Radon Gas

Ely is a small but growing community of approximately 2,000 residents located just south of Cedar Rapids in Linn County, Iowa. Positioned along the Prairie Creek Greenway Trail and just minutes from the Cedar Rapids metro, Ely offers residents a peaceful, rural setting with easy access to city conveniences. The town has seen a wave of new residential construction in recent years as families seek affordable homes with generous lot sizes in the highly rated Prairie Creek Community School District. Despite its small size, Ely faces the same significant radon risk as every other community in Linn County — the EPA's highest-risk Zone 1 classification means the majority of homes in Ely are expected to have radon concentrations that exceed safe levels.

The terrain around Ely features gently rolling agricultural land underlain by carbonate bedrock and thick glacial till — soil conditions that are particularly efficient at generating and channeling radon gas toward the surface. Homes built on full basements, which are standard in this part of Iowa, provide the pressure differential that draws radon-laden soil gas indoors. Even newer construction in Ely's growing subdivisions can have significant radon problems because modern homes tend to be built tighter for energy efficiency, which reduces natural ventilation and allows radon to accumulate to higher concentrations. Our team installs active soil depressurization systems specifically designed for Ely's soil and construction conditions, using high-quality radon fans and sealed PVC piping to create continuous sub-slab suction that prevents radon from entering your home.

For Ely homeowners, radon mitigation is both a health investment and a smart financial decision. Iowa real estate transactions increasingly require radon testing, and having a professionally installed mitigation system with documented post-mitigation results below 4.0 pCi/L adds value to your property and eliminates a common deal-breaker during home sales. Our systems are installed with care to minimize visual impact — we route piping through closets, utility rooms, or along the exterior of the home to maintain your property's curb appeal. Each installation includes a system monitor gauge so you can verify at a glance that your system is operating correctly.

We proudly serve Ely and the surrounding southern Linn County area including Cedar Rapids, Fairfax, Swisher, and Solon. As a locally operated company, we understand the unique building practices and soil conditions found in small Iowa communities like Ely. Our technicians arrive on time, treat your home with respect, and complete most installations within a single day. We are licensed and insured, and every mitigation system we install includes a post-mitigation radon test to confirm effectiveness. Contact us today for a free estimate and take the first step toward cleaner indoor air for your Ely home.
