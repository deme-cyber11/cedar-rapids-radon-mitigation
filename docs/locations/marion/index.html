Radon Mitigation Services for Marion Homeowners

Marion is one of the fastest-growing cities in Linn County, with a population of more than 40,000 residents and a thriving mix of established neighborhoods and brand-new subdivisions. Situated directly adjacent to Cedar Rapids on the northeast side, Marion has seen significant residential development over the past two decades — from the Lowe Park area to the Prospect Meadows corridor and the newer developments off Highway 13. Like the rest of Linn County, Marion sits within EPA Radon Zone 1, where predicted average indoor radon levels exceed 4 pCi/L. The glacial deposits and fractured limestone bedrock beneath Marion create natural pathways for radon gas to migrate upward from the soil and into homes through foundation cracks, sump pits, and construction joints.

Our radon mitigation team has extensive experience working in Marion's diverse housing stock. Whether your home is a mid-century ranch near Thomas Park, a split-level in the Indian Creek neighborhood, or a newly built two-story in Bowman Woods, we design and install custom active soil depressurization systems tailored to your specific foundation type and soil conditions. Our technicians begin every project with a detailed site evaluation, measuring sub-slab communication, identifying radon entry points, and determining the optimal suction point placement. Most residential installations in Marion are completed in four to six hours, allowing you to return to a safer home the same day.

Marion's position in the Cedar Rapids metropolitan area means many families here commute to work in neighboring communities while enjoying a quieter, family-oriented lifestyle. That same family focus is why radon testing should be a priority for every Marion household. The Iowa Department of Public Health reports that approximately seven out of ten Iowa homes tested have radon levels above the EPA action level. Radon exposure is the second leading cause of lung cancer in the United States, and because the gas is colorless and odorless, professional testing is the only way to know if your home is affected. We recommend testing every two years, after any major renovation, or whenever you purchase a new property in Marion.

We serve all of Marion and the surrounding Linn County communities including Cedar Rapids, Hiawatha, Center Point, and Mount Vernon. Our local presence means we can typically schedule your radon test or mitigation installation within days, not weeks. Every system we install comes with a post-mitigation test to verify performance and ensure your home's radon levels are well below the EPA guideline. Call us today for a free estimate and protect your Marion home from radon gas.
