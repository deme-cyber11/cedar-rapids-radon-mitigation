Comprehensive Radon Mitigation for Solon Homes

Solon is a welcoming community of approximately 3,000 residents in Johnson County, Iowa, strategically positioned between Cedar Rapids to the north and Iowa City to the south along Highway 1. This location makes Solon a popular choice for families and professionals who commute to either city while enjoying the benefits of small-town life — a top-rated school district, safe neighborhoods, and a strong sense of community. The town's residential development has expanded steadily, with new subdivisions complementing Solon's well-established older neighborhoods. However, Johnson County shares the same radon risk profile as neighboring Linn County, falling within EPA Zone 1 where predicted average indoor radon levels are above 4 pCi/L. For Solon homeowners, understanding and addressing radon exposure is a critical part of responsible homeownership.

Solon sits within the Iowa River valley region, where the underlying geology features Silurian and Devonian limestone covered by layers of glacial drift and loess deposits. These geological formations are naturally enriched with uranium, the parent element of radon gas. As uranium decays deep within the bedrock, radon is released into the surrounding soil, where it migrates upward through pore spaces, fractures, and any available pathway toward the surface. When a home sits above this soil, the slightly lower air pressure inside the building — created by heating systems, exhaust fans, and the natural stack effect — actively draws radon gas through the foundation. In Solon, where full basements are the norm and winters require months of sealed-up living, indoor radon can reach concentrations that pose serious long-term health risks.

Our radon mitigation systems are specifically designed to counteract these conditions. Using active soil depressurization technology, we install a network of sealed PVC piping that extends through the basement slab to the soil below, connected to a high-efficiency radon fan that creates continuous negative pressure beneath the foundation. This prevents radon from entering the home by redirecting the gas through the pipe system and exhausting it above the roofline. For Solon homes with crawl spaces or unique foundation configurations, we also offer sub-membrane depressurization and other specialized solutions. Every system is installed by our trained technicians in compliance with EPA and ASTM guidelines, and we include a post-mitigation radon test to verify that your home's levels have been reduced to safe concentrations.

Solon's location between Cedar Rapids and Iowa City means residents benefit from our central service position — we can typically schedule appointments within a few days and complete most installations in a single visit. We also serve neighboring Johnson County communities including North Liberty, Coralville, and Iowa City, as well as Linn County towns to the north. Whether you are buying a home in Solon's newest subdivision, selling your existing property, or simply want peace of mind about your family's indoor air quality, professional radon testing is the essential first step. We are licensed and insured and offer free estimates for all Solon homeowners. Call us today.
