Iowa's Radon Risk — And What You Can Do About It

Iowa has some of the highest radon concentrations in the United States, and the Cedar Rapids area is no exception. Radon is a naturally occurring radioactive gas that forms from the decay of uranium in soil and rock. It seeps into homes through foundation cracks, slab joints, and utility penetrations — and because it's colorless and odorless, you can't detect it without a proper test.

The health consequences of long-term radon exposure are well-documented: it's the second leading cause of lung cancer in the United States, responsible for approximately 21,000 deaths annually. The good news is that modern radon mitigation systems are highly effective — reducing indoor radon levels by up to 99% in most cases.

Five Seasons Radon provides NRPP-certified radon testing and mitigation throughout the Cedar Rapids metro. If you haven't tested your home, now is the time. If your test came back elevated, we can have a system installed and working within days. Contact us for a free estimate — protecting your family starts with a single call.
